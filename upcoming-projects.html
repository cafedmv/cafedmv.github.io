Chalk Art Event

July 27, 2019 @ ACCA Pop-Up Park

We want to inspire smiles into Annandale's newest pop-up park to bring our Annandale community closer together.

This project will be a cooperation consisting of local artists, residents, and CAFE volunteers. The activities will foster connections amongst the participants and within the community.
