The "Talk Concert": The Korean Lady Next Door

June 7th @ Stay C. Sherwood Community Center

To celebrate the arrival of the touring exhibition coming to Fairfax, "We Didn’t Know How to Write, but We Knew How to Live ", you're invited to the "Talk Concert": The Korean Lady Next Door. This event will be an enlightening gathering which features three Korean women living in the United States who will share their fascinating stories, and an American jazz vocalist singing a traditional Korean folk song.

Public Piano Project

June ??, 2019 @ Stay C. Sherwood Community Center

We will inspire the image of “smile” into the pop-up park and the participants will develop an affection for the Annandale area.

Our community art activities have three parts; as mural art, a public piano, and a chalk art event.
