Volunteer

We're going to need alot of help to bring change to our community, and we hope you can join in too. You don't need any artistic experience- or any creative talent either. Instead, we'll need energy, passion and a love for bringing life and art to local communities.

We're currently looking for:

  • Event volunteers to help facilitate workshops, teach children, or set up/take down props.
  • Media gurus who know how to take pics and hashtag like an influencer. The more awareness we can get, the more support we'll receive to do what we do best.
  • Artists and creatives to help us lead workshops, ideate projects, or just paint/draw/create awesome art on public canvases for the community to enjoy.
  • And anyone else with a passion for community to help bring the energy to our community art projects and events.
